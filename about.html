Our Heritage

1993

The First Oven

It began in a small cobblestone alley. With nothing but a wood-fired oven and a passion for yeast, we baked our first loaf. Every detail was meticulously observed, setting the standard for everything that followed.

2005

Mastering Sourdough

Our signature wild yeast starter was born, giving our breads an unparalleled complex flavor that locals line up for. We firmly believe that good bread cannot be rushed.

TODAY

An Artisan Legacy

Today we are recognized internationally, yet we still craft every single loaf entirely by hand. The legacy of La Pâtisserie continues with every golden crust we bake.
